Our Mission

Speed Wagon was born from a passion for exploring the unknown. Our goal is to bring the wonders of the deep ocean to everyone, combining science, technology and design.

We believe that knowledge of the sea is essential to understanding our planet. Every layer of the ocean hides secrets that deserve to be discovered and shared.

Our Story

Speed Wagon started as a student project with a clear vision: to create a digital platform that connected people with the fascinating world of marine creatures.

Since its beginnings, the team has worked with passion to build a unique experience that blends education, design and modern technology with Angular and Bootstrap.
